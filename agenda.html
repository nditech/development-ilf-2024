Agenda

Attendees at ILF2024 will participate in a robust agenda that includes panels, networking opportunities, and specialized luncheons, capped off with an evening watch party and direct observation of Convention proceedings. All event venues are situated near the United Center and DNC daytime activities at McCormick Place convention center.

Agenda updates and session descriptions will appear here as they take shape.
