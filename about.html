WHAT IS THE INTERNATIONAL LEADERS FORUM?

The International Leaders Forum provides ambassadors to the United States an opportunity to observe the convention proceedings and take part in a series of bipartisan panel discussions on the U.S. political process, and foreign and domestic policy issues. They also have a rare opportunity to exchange ideas informally while getting a unique perspective on an American national political convention.

WHAT IS THE NATIONAL DEMOCRATIC INSTITUTE?

NDI is a nonprofit, nonpartisan, non-governmental organization that works for democracy and makes democracy work in 70 countries. NDI responds to the aspirations of people around the world to live in democratic societies that recognize and promote fundamental human rights. NDI receives no funding from any political party and is not an arm of the Democratic Party.

WHEN AND WHERE IS ILF?

The 2020 International Leaders Forum will be held virtually August 17-20.

WHO IS INVITED?

Invitees include ambassadors from over 100 countries, members of Parliament, political party leaders, ministers, and other international partners from the countries where NDI operates.
