Attendees

Attendance at ILF is by NDI invitation only.

Your registration covers -

  • Meals and transportation to forum events once the program begins (i.e. opening reception on Monday to final watch party on Thursday);
  • Access to educational and networking opportunities with other global leaders such as panels, presentations and global conversations;
  • Access to credentials for at least one night of the Democratic National Convention;
  • Discount airfare if using the Lufthansa Group Airlines, the Preferred Airline Partners for ILF2024.

Support the Next Generation of Leaders

Participants are responsible for their own travel to the event location and lodging costs.

Due to the high demand that the Convention places on the city of Chicago and the unique logistics of our event, participants are required to stay at the event hotel - Hyatt Regency McCormick Place. The cost per night is $399 (plus tax) for a standard room. There will be a required minimum stay at hotel. Lodging and airfare instructions will be provided once your registration is complete.

See registration emails for more information on arriving in Chicago and what you can expect during ILF.
