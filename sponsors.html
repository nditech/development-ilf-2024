Interested in Sponsoring ILF 2024?

The International Leaders Forum 2024 (ILF) is the only forum where leaders of the international community can observe the dynamic workings of U.S. democracy in action.

This gathering of global leaders is a rare opportunity to interface with such a broadly diverse set of stakeholders, thought leaders, and decision-makers. National and global corporations and organizations such as yours will have the chance to engage with the very people responsible for economic and political decision-making, creating global opportunities, and supporting democratic values.

Join NDI and the international community as we further democracy through observation, reflection, and dialogue.

While ILF will occur adjacent to the Democratic National Convention, NDI is a non-partisan organization and ILF's participation in the Convention is meant to be illustrative of the convention experience in the U.S. election system, not an endorsement of a particular candidate or political party.
